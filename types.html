Types of Guitars

Acoustic

Dreadnaught Guitar: Has a bigger body to allow for a louder vibration

Classical Guitar: Has a medium sized body. Typically seen with nylon strings and fingerstyle playing

Concert Guitar: Smaller than dreadnaught body and better for solo playing

Electric

Solid-body Guitar: your typical electric guitar that using electromagnetic pickup

Stratocaster Guitar: Has been played by icons like Jimi Hendrix for more jazzy music

Les Paul Guitar: A type of solid body guitar that has low string tension making it easier to play
