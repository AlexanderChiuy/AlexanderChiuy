Jazz History

Jazz music was a very influential genre in the 60s and 70s. Its origins started in African-American communities and was largely improv. One of the special jazz icons was, Jimi Hendrix, a self taught guitarists. He is reffered to as one of the most influential guitarists of all time. Jimi Hendrix overcame many obstacles during his career, once of these obstacles being that he was left-handed in a world where there was only right-handed guitars.
